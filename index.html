The Solar System

Our solar system is the local region in space consisting of our sun and all the neighboring planets that, like Earth, are bound to it by gravity. It consists of 8 planets, along with many other objects such as moons, meteors, satellites amongst others.

The Sun

The sun is the centerpiece of the solar system, it is the biggest object and provides the light which makes all other planets within the solar system visible. It also provides the energy that gives all living organisms on earth life.

The Moon

The moon is an integral piece of Earth's complex orbiting system, which helps stabilize it. On top of this, the satellite influences the tides, our timekeeping, and though not the only one in the solar system it is affects Earth more than any other.
